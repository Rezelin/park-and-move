BEFORE YOU SWEAT

SAFETY GUIDELINES

🚗 Vehicle Safety — Non-Negotiable

  • Your vehicle must be in PARK before starting any routine
  • Never use this app while driving or with the vehicle in motion
  • For out-of-car routines, ensure you are parked in a safe, open area away from traffic
  • Be aware of your surroundings before stepping out of the vehicle
  • If you are on a road shoulder or unsafe location, stay in the car

🏃 Know Your Body, Know Your Limits

  • Stop immediately if you feel pain, dizziness, shortness of breath, or discomfort
  • Consult a doctor or healthcare professional before starting any new exercise routine
  • These exercises are low-intensity but still carry risk — listen to your body
  • If you are recovering from an injury or have a medical condition, check with your doctor first
  • Wear appropriate footwear for out-of-car exercises

☀️ Environmental Awareness

  • Avoid intense activity in extreme heat — risk of heat exhaustion is real
  • Stay hydrated — keep water in your vehicle
  • In cold weather, warm up slower and avoid icy surfaces for out-of-car exercises
  • If exercising near traffic, make yourself visible and stay alert
  • Do not block roadways, driveways, or emergency access when parked
